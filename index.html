A Place To Call Home

If you live in the Stokesdale area we invite you join us for worship and fellowship as we focus our attention on the Word of God.

Plan A Visit

Plan A Visit

Visiting a new church can be an intimidating experience. We want to put you at ease by answering the most common questions people have….

What are Sunday worship services like?

After a time of fellowship, we start our service with prayer. As a congregation we sing hymns and choruses. There is special music offered to the Lord as worship. We take up an offering (visitors are not expected to give) and then the pastor or a special speaker will preach from the Scriptures.

What are Wednesday evenings like?

After a time of fellowship we pray and then we teach very basic and practical truths from the Scriptures. Questions are encouraged and answered after the study. Wednesday evenings are more casual than Sunday mornings. Often we have a snack and a cup of coffee.

When do you meet?

Sundays:

Sunday School - 10:00AM
Morning Service - 11:00AM

Wednesdays

Bible Study - 7:00 PM

What should I wear?

We have no dress code. We only ask that you wear something that is comfortable but not distracting to those around you.

Is there anything for my kids?

We provide the following for children:

  • Nursery is provided for children age 5 and under.
  • A Sunday school for elementary aged kids.
  • Wednesday youth programs coming soon.
  • Occasional youth activities for teenagers

Am I expected to give?

No. We do take an offering every service, but it is for our members. We do not expect our guests to give anything. You’ve given us enough by visiting.

What do you believe?

We believe in the inspiration and inerrancy --of scriptures, the trinity of the godhead, the virgin birth of Christ, the death, burial, and resurrection of Jesus Christ, that salvation is by grace through faith in Christ’s finished work on Calvary, and the return of Christ.
